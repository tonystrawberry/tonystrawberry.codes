To be honest, I could have skipped this one to pass the Solutions Architect Associate but I prefered to go slowly but surely ğŸ¢ and gain confidence in my abilities. I won't say I didn't learn anything new though. I learned about many other services like AWS Organizations for centralizing the billing of multiple AWS accounts, AWS Snow family for helping migrate data from on-premises data center to the cloud and many other. You are not required to have very deep knowledge of the AWS services but you will need to learn about a lot of them.

I studied approximately 1 month using the acloudguru.com website and I can say it covered everything I needed to know in order to pass the examination without issues.
